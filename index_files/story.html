Axl’s story, in 2 ways...

| Created by Axl Woodland | Last Update: September 29th, 2023 |

For evaluation purposes only

                                    I think it’s time I let you guys know how I became the cuddly wolf you (don’t) see before you. It could have been better, but have you ever tried remembering all those years?.. Alright, let’s start...

 

The Origins (2007? - 2017)

So, I have been drawing for almost as long as I've been alive. I never liked my drawings then, until they decided to take a turn in 2017. At this time, I was drawing Sonic art and had the online identity of “Crimson”.  Crimson was a sonic OC (fox) at this time.

 

The end of the Crimson era (2017-2019)

My art motivation was drooping here, but I had then had stopped for a few years, completely abandoning my profile and the Crimson side, due to depression from bullying taking it’s toll. I had literally disappeared without warning, despite the great friends I had made then. Sometime in early 2018, I began putting research into what a furry was, due to people throwing around the term in my school. At the time I had zero idea what a furry was. I came across some fursuit images, and found them cute!

 

Flicking the switch again (2019 – 2020)

Then came mid-2019, I started learning to draw properly again, having forgotten how to properly draw, severely impacting the quality of my drawings as a result. Still having never been able to draw with poses, I had used Garry’s Mod, with a Barney Calhoun ragdoll to help make my poses. At the time, I thought my art was getting better, So, in the early days of lockdown (2020), I worked up the courage to try out becoming a furry artist, this time making my new side a complete secret persona, hidden to the people I knew in real life.

 

 

The Prototype Axl (2020-2021)

Having made this account, I only came up with a simple white wolf with zero special details. This was of course not very good in the slightest. I needed something more creative. Still going with the wolf, I decided to make it light gray with blobs of white. Perfect, but not enough! So I decided to make him some sort of cyborg. I made one of his eyes a monochrome screen, that changes colour to his feelings. He himself needed a few weapons to support the eye. I never actually decided on said weapons, but he had them! He also had an entire sort of back-story, being a failed experiment as a weapon. All this needed what I thought to have been a bad-ass name to go with it! Axl! At this time I had also conjured up designs for Justine, and more of my characters! Some you see in the banner below!

 

Axl 2.0 (2021-2022)

That “Failed Experimental Weapon” concept, however, started to get old fast, so I decided to revamp Axl’s look! Removing those parts about him, making him a normal wolf. I wanted to make his look more unique too, so I darkened the gray on his fur, and replaced the simple tuft of fur on his head with a hairstyle, sporting crimson highlights.  To support the hair, I also added a crimson stripe to his tail.

 

A Back To The Future level comeback. (2022)

People seemed to be liking my art, but the more I did this method, I was losing my motivation with art very fast. This was getting worse and worse, and right when I was going to quit again, my computer stopped working. I was then forced to actually learn how to pose properly, so I tried and tried, and I finally got the gist of it. Somehow, that started to give me much more motivation than the motivation I already lost!

 

A more friendly (and magical) approach (2022-E.2023)

Axl still being a normal wolf, needed some peculiar features to distinguish him from other wolves, along as the other characters I made! I overtime decided to give Axl the ability to purr, and I made his tail magical, allowing him to grow and extend the tail to great sizes and lengths! To support this, I made his personality a LOT more friendly! Justine also had the ability to control water!

 

Taking off once more (E.2023 - M.2023)

I started getting used to this new posing, and over time, my art gradually became better, and better! I had started experimenting with animation at this time too! That was until I moved to drawing on my laptop, that’s when things really started to take off! Some really cool dude called Cyus came along and helped me improve my posing sketches! Now people I draw are much more better thanks to him! I was also helped by a different person, allowing me to draw necks, and shade better! Around this time,

 

The Viona Era (2023)

Once the whole “Failed Experiment” back-story was phased out, I made my characters an actual alien race called “Vionans”, obviously on the planet Viona. I also made Axl and Justine spacecraft pilots! Axl would pilot the Eclipse, a really feisty ship that can take and give one hell of a beating, and Justine would pilot the Dragonfly, a ship similar to the Eclipse, but it’s style was based entirely off of a dragonfly. Around this time, was when my skill with animation REALLY took off! This was because I had started using a program specifically designed for animation!
