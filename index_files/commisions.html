Working Days

To help prevent me from getting overworked I may only work on agreed Commissions, Trades and Requests on certain days. Outside of these working days, I will take a break until the new “week” begins. Only personal art will be worked on at this time. It will also help contain my on-going distraction issues.


These working days are:

Monday-Wednesday: May be Inconsistent. Depends on the day. (College)

Thursday and Friday: I will work on them if I have the energy.

Saturday and Sunday: Personal art only.

-Me posting the result to any online platforms (Though I will always ask before doing this regardless)

As the topic suggests, I’ll do my best to keep you anonymous. You are free to ask me to post it, but I will put “Artwork for an anonymous user” instead of “Artwork for (Your Screen name)”
